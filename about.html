Biography

I grew up in Gaithersburg, MD, about 30 minutes north of Washington D.C. I studied at the University of Maryland and graduated in May 2020 with a B.S. in Information Science with a specialization in Data Science, and with a B.S. in Psychology. My current interests are in Data Science, UI/UX Design, and using big data to gain insights in civil rights issues.

I am very interested in the intersection between technology and people, and what the implications of an ever-growing interconnected world has on this relationship. I am also passionate about Climate Change, Science, and Science Communication. Before becoming an Information Science and Psychology major, I was taking environmental science courses. One day, I hope to merge these two (or three) subjects to improve people's lives and promote sustainabiliity.

In my free time, I enjoy hanging out with friends, trying new foods, cooking, playing video games, and photography
