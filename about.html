Biography

I am a student at the University of Maryland. I'm currently in my junior year studying Information Science and Psychology. I'll be graduating in Spring 2020 with a B.S. in both majors. I grew up in Gaithersburg, MD, about 30 minutes north of Washington D.C.

I am very interested in the relationship between computers and people, and the implications of an every growing interconnected world has on this. I am also passionate about Climate Change, Science, and Science Communication. Before becoming an Information Science and Psychology major, I was taking environmental science courses. One day, I hope to merge these two (or three) subjects to improve people's lives and promote sustainabiliity.

In my free time, I enjoy hanging out with friends, trying new foods, cooking, playing video games, and photography
